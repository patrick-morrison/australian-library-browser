SWAN RIVER RACES

New Boat Christened

THE SWAN RIVER ROWING Club conducted races yesterday afternoon for the Hobbs Fours. Opportunity was taken to christen the new racing eight, named the "W. Kirby," as a token to Mr. W. J. Kirby. The race for the Hobbs Fours was contested in five heats, a semi-final, and a final. Results: — First Heat: F. Pow beat W. Peacock. Second Heat: A. Wilson beat C. Wooltit. Third Heat: B. Ridley beat C. Henderson. Fourth heat: S. Gorringe beat H. Coombe.

Fifth heat: J. Aspinall beat C. Henderson. Semi-finals: Pow beat Gorringe, Ridley beat Woolfit. and Aspinall received the bye. Final: J. Aspinall (stroke), A. Scott, and J. Coronel Leat F. Pow (stroke), P. Gard, C. Cohn and J. Kean. D. Ridley (stroke) Bridgart. W. Casson and S. Power were compelled to retire owing to a broken seat.
