WELLINGTON DAM SCHEME

NARROMINE, Friday: The Mac- quarie Water Conservation and Irri- gation League, with which Local Gov- ernment bodies and other organisa- tions in an area from Wellington to Nyngan are affiliated, will meet at Narromine on Tuesday, August 30, to consider the question of the utilisa- tion of the waters impounded by the

Burrendong Dam. A special committee appointed by the Minister for Conservation will at the early date inquire into this mat- ter and the purpose of the meeting is to determine the policy of the league and authorise the chin1,1 of formal evidence at the inquiry.
