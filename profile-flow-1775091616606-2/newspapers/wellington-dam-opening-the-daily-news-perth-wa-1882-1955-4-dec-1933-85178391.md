WELLINGTON DAM OPENING *

The Wellington reservoir, which has a capacity of 7,500,000,000 gallons, and Collie River irrigation scheme will be officially opened by the Minister for Works (Mr. M'Callum) on Saturday

next. An idea of the huge capacity of the new dam is afforded by a comparison with Mundaring Weir, which holds 4,650,000,000 gallons. The opening will take place at 11 a.m.. and at 1 p.m. a luncheon will be ten dered the Minister to mark the event, at Brunswick Junction.. The Harvey Roads Board is co-operating with the Collie Municipal Council and Collie and Dardanup roads boards in the arrange ments.
